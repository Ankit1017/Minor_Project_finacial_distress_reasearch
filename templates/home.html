Financial Distress Analyser

Enhanced Bat Algorithm for Feature Reduction

The Enhanced Bat Algorithm is an advanced technique for reducing the dimensionality of financial datasets. By selecting the most relevant features, it improves the predictive accuracy of financial distress models while reducing computational overhead. This algorithm ensures that only the most informative features are used, leading to better decision-making and more efficient data processing.

Deep Q-Network (DQN) Model

The Deep Q-Network (DQN) model is designed to predict financial distress by learning optimal trading strategies through a neural network. By using a reward-based system, it evaluates trading actions and iteratively improves its strategy, aiming to maximize financial gains and minimize risks. The DQN model helps stakeholders make informed decisions by identifying potential financial distress early on.
